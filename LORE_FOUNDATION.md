# Elderfield — Lore Foundation (updated through v2.7.1)

## Core Named Pillars
- **Kingdom:** Elderfield
- **Princess:** Elaria Vale
- **Ancient Evil:** the Briar King
- **Forgotten Order:** the Dawn Wardens
- **Bloodline:** the Aurel line
- **Sacred Shrine:** the Dawn Shrine

## Why These Matter
These names are the first permanent mythic anchors for the game. Future items, dungeons, NPCs, and story beats should grow outward from them instead of feeling random.

## Relics Introduced So Far
- **Aurel Crest** — a sacred crest tied to blade-memory and royal oath
- **Galespine Spear** — a wind-and-root relic tied to hidden roads and warded crossings
- **Cinderwake Wand** — a sleeping-flame relic tied to protective ember seals

## Dragon Thread
- **Vaelor the High Wyrm** should represent wisdom, memory, and ancient guardianship.
- **Cindervane** should represent danger, loss, corruption, or divided dragonfire.

## Story Direction Going Forward
Princess Elaria is not merely kidnapped. She has been hidden within the **Thornglass Sleep**, a warded state tied to the old relic roads and the return of the Briar King. The hero's connection to the **Aurel line** and the **Dawn Wardens** should unfold gradually through relic responses, NPC recognition, tablets, and dragon lore.

## Next Lore Targets
- the capital city and royal house
- the first wise NPC who knows Elaria's fate
- the castle/temple where the Thornglass Sleep was wrought
- the first real reveal of what the Briar King wants
- how Vaelor and Cindervane are tied to the old war
